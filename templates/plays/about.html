About Rate A Leg

Welcome to Rate a Leg! We are a team of five developers with a passion for shows and theatre. We designed this website for like-minded people to easily navigate their show/play needs! We love the theatre world and want to share it with anyone new to the scene (or old!).

What We Provide

This website allows you to search and sort for your local and favourite shows and even review and rate them if you are a registered user! Whether you're new to the theatre scene or a seasoned veteran, we have something for everyone! You can also see all your local Glasgow theatres to plan a day of fun and check out where shows that you want to watch are being shown.

Our Mission

We want to join communities together, making everyone's opinion known and experiences heard. We want your help to maintain and build upon our website from your feedback. If you have any feedback, feel free to contact us here.
